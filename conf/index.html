I'm safe

Disaster strikes! You have no Internet or cell service. You are safe but unable to tell your family and friends outside of the disaster area. We can help!

You can compose a short message and we'll send that message, via ham (amateur) radio to a location with Internet access.

First, choose how you want us to deliver your message, via Email or via SMS?

You can also download a template spreadsheet file.

Edit the template spreadsheet, adding new messages. When you are ready, upload using the Browse button below.
