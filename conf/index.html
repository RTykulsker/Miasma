I'm safe

Disaster strikes! You have no Internet or cell service. You are safe but unable to let your family and friends outside of the disaster area know that. We can help!

If you compose a short message, we'll send that message, via ham (amateur) radio for delivery to locations with Internet (email) and/or celluar (SMS/text message) access.

You can compose your message here

compose

OR

You can also download a template spreadsheet file.

Edit the template spreadsheet, adding new messages. When you are ready, upload using the submit button below.

Note that message delivery is NOT guaranteed and replies to your device are NOT possible
